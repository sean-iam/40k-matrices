Drukhari Melee Matrix

Four melee bricks, one army running both detachments (11e). A = 10 Incubi + Archon · B = 5 Incubi + Lady Malys · C = 10 Wyches + Lelith · D = 5 Incubi + Drazhar. Everyone gets Skysplinter Assault (Lance on disembark+charge, all Drukhari) and Exhibition of Slaughter (Lethal Hits, Wych Cult only). Infantry = models killed (overkill wasted); vehicles = wounds dealt. Melee only — pistols excluded.

Modelling & assumptions — read before trusting a number
  • Empower applies BOTH Pain abilities (leader + bodyguard) — all are in the math, the card just abbreviates: A = Hatred Eternal (re-roll Hits, Archon) + Decapitating Strikes (Devastating vs INFANTRY, Incubi). B = Poisoned Tongue (Sustained 1 or Lethal, auto-picked, Lady Malys) + Decapitating Strikes (Devastating vs INFANTRY, Incubi). C = Brides of Death (+1 Strength & +1 AP, Lelith) + Acrobatic Gladiators (Wyches — charge after Advance/Fall Back; mobility only, no damage, so nothing to add). D = Master of Blades (+1 to the Wound roll, whole unit, Drazhar) + Decapitating Strikes (Devastating vs INFANTRY, Incubi). Devastating shows on the card only vs INFANTRY, where it applies.
  • Build D — Drazhar + 5 Incubi: the 5 Incubi are the same brick as build B (4 klaives A3 S5 AP-2 D2 + 1 Klaivex single demiklaive A4). Drazhar's Executioner's demiklaives have native Devastating Wounds on both profiles, so he keeps mortal-wound output even into vehicles. Two mutually-exclusive profiles — pick ONE per fight (don't read both totals at once): single blade A6 S6 AP-2 D3 (default on; better into elites/vehicles) vs dual blades A8 S5 AP-2 D2 (toggle on, dual off; better into hordes). His empower is the only one that buffs wounding for the whole unit, so it doesn't stack with Lance's +1 wound (both cap at +1).
  • Drazhar's Silent Executioner — two toggles (Drazhar's blades only, not the Incubi): re-roll Hits fires when the target is below its Starting Strength; re-roll Wounds is added on top when the target is Below Half-strength (in-game the wound re-roll always comes bundled with the hit re-roll — toggle both for the below-half case). Both are off by default because they depend on target state — the matrix targets start full-strength, so this is Drazhar's ceiling into an already-chewed-up unit, not his alpha strike. Onslaught (6" pile-in/consolidate) is mobility only, no damage.
  • Incubi Shrine token(s): once per game per token you may change one Klaivex or Incubi (not the leader) Hit or Wound roll to an unmodified 6 — ×2 in a 10-Incubi unit (A), ×1 in a 5-Incubi unit (B/D), none for Wyches (C). Toggle on to count the wound use: one guaranteed critical wound on an Incubi klaive (S5 AP-2 D2), shown as its own ⬡ Shrine token row. It's a mortal wound vs INFANTRY when Empowered (Decapitating Strikes → bypasses saves), otherwise a saveable wound. Additive once-per-game estimate (assumes you convert an otherwise-failed roll); the Hit-roll use isn't modelled separately.
  • Exhibition of Slaughter (build C): Exacting Cruelty gives Wych Cult melee Lethal Hits vs non-Monster/Vehicle (i.e. vs Marines/Terminators, always). For vehicles you need Planned Strikes (1CP) → Lethal vs anything. No damage enhancements in the detachment.
  • Lelith's blades: A8, Anti-Infantry 2+ (wounds infantry on 2+), Sustained Hits 2, Precision. Hekatrix on gladiatorial weapons (A5 S4 AP-2); 9 Wyches on Hekatarii blades (A4 S3 AP-1 D1).
  • Archon Soul Trap baked in (huskblade A5/S4); on-kill ramp and Lelith's Precision (character sniping) not modelled — they don't change these target numbers.
  • 11e multi-detachment: the army runs both Skysplinter Assault + Exhibition of Slaughter, and every unit uses both — limited by keyword. Lance (+1 wound on disembark+charge) = all Drukhari (A/B/C). Exacting Cruelty / Planned Strikes (Lethal) = Wych Cult only (C). Tormentors (+1 hit vs Battle-shocked) = Incubi units (A/B/D). Skysplinter's stratagems are ranged/movement/defensive, so they add no melee-damage lever.
  • Devastating = mortal wounds, bypass saves. Overkill wasted on infantry (capped at unit size); vehicles show raw expected wounds + ONE-ROUNDS at ≥ W. Hit/wound modifiers cap at ±1; re-rolls stack on top. Cover is ranged-only, inert here.
  • Confirm: that Lelith can lead Wyches and Lady Malys can lead Incubi in your lists — the math holds either way, but those are the legality points I didn't verify.
