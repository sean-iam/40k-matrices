Modelling & assumptions — read before trusting a number
  • Ranged only. No shearing claws / electroscourge.
  • Detachment rule is baked in per side: Soulforged = +1 wound, Pactbound = crit on 5+ (sus/lethal). Toggle the master switch up top to compare.
  • One stratagem per unit per phase (11e) — that's why Stratagem is single-select, not stacking toggles.
  • Stratagems (audited from the 10e CSM codex): the only ranged-damage strat usable here is Desperate Pledge (SF, +1 AP). Pactbound's Profane Zeal (re-roll wounds) needs the Chaos Undivided mark, incompatible with the crit-on-5 (Tzeentch/Nurgle) build you run, so it's excluded. All other strats in both detachments are melee / healing / movement / defensive. 11e may have errata'd some — verify.
  • Enhancements (audited from source): only Soulforged's Tempting Addendum raises the Defiler's shooting (re-roll Hits — it's the "Re-roll hits" toggle). No other enhancement in either detachment touches ranged output (rest are CP / defense / movement / melee).
  • Pact = Off / Sustained / Lethal / Both (Hellbrutes). Crit-on-5 feeds Sustained & Lethal triggers; Devastating crit-wound kept at 6.
  • Devastating Wounds (reaper) = mortal wounds = Damage, bypassing all saves (max one model/crit).
  • Sustained does NOT stack — reaper's native Sustained 1 + Sustained pact = 1. Override if your FAQ differs.
  • Lethal optional per attack (auto-picked vs keeping Devastating). Blast = +2 attacks vs 10-model infantry, none vs vehicles.
  • Overkill wasted on infantry (carry within a model, killing-blow overkill lost), capped at 10. Vehicles = raw expected wounds; ONE-ROUNDS at ≥ model W.
