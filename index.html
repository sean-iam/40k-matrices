Damage / Matrices

Interactive Warhammer 40,000 damage comparisons. Each tool pits two builds against the same four targets — flip buffs, stratagems, marks and weapons and watch what actually dies. Numbers are expected values; assumptions are listed in each tool.

How to read them. Pick a target tab (Marines / Terminators / Vehicle / Heavy Tank). Infantry shows models killed (overkill wasted); vehicles show wounds dealt, flagging when a build one-rounds it. Toggle any buff to recompute instantly. Open the “Modelling & assumptions” note at the bottom of each tool to see exactly how it's calculated.
