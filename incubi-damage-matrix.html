Modelling & assumptions — read before trusting a number
  • Empower (Power From Pain): spending 1 Pain token Empowers the unit. Build A (Archon): Hatred Eternal → re-roll Hits. Build B (Lady Malys): Poisoned Tongue → Sustained 1 or Lethal (tool auto-picks the higher per target, shown on the card). Both also get Decapitating Strikes: vs INFANTRY only, every melee attack gains Devastating Wounds (= mortal wounds, bypass saves).
  • Archon Soul Trap is baked in: huskblade shown at A5 / S4 (base 4/3 +1/+1). The on-kill ramp is not modelled.
  • Tormentors = +1 to Hit vs a Battle-shocked unit (Incubi force the test at fight start). Toggle = assume the target failed it.
  • Rain of Cruelty (detachment): a unit that disembarked gets Lance on melee = +1 to Wound on a turn it charged. Toggle = disembarked and charged.
  • Klaivex modelled with the single-blade demiklaive (A4 S5 AP-2 D2). Dual blades (A6 S4 AP-1 D1, twin-linked) are better into hordes — say the word and I'll swap or add it.
  • Devastating native on Archon huskblade and Lady's Blade always (even vs vehicles). Cover is −1 hit, ranged only, so it's inert here. Hazardous on Lady's Blade (self-risk) isn't an output factor.
  • Overkill wasted on infantry (capped at unit size); vehicles show raw expected wounds + ONE-ROUNDS at ≥ W. Hit/wound modifiers cap at ±1; re-rolls stack on top.
